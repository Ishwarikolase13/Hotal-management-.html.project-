An AC room is a space that has its temperature controlled by an air-conditioning system, which cools, dehumidifies, heats, and filters the air. Key information for an AC room includes selecting the correct AC capacity (tonnage) based on the room's square footage, prioritizing energy efficiency with a high star rating, and considering features like inverter technology and smart controls for better performance and convenience.
